ROS Package for Rhino DC Servo Motor

Summary

In the final year of my undergrad, I decided to develop a differential-drive robot from scratch. I found that there is no ROS compatible motor in India to use in my project. So I decided to select a widely available motor in India to use in my project. I developed a ROS package for that motor and released it as open-source in my github page. My work got recognized by the sole dealer and they offered me with credits on their website.
